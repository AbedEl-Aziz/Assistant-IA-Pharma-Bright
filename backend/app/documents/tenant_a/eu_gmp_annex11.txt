TITLE: EU GMP Annex 11 – Computerised Systems

Section 1: Risk Management
Risk management should be applied throughout the lifecycle of the computerized system.

Section 2: Personnel
Personnel should have appropriate qualifications and training.

Section 3: Data Integrity
Data should be secure, accurate, and protected against unauthorized access.

Section 4: Audit Trails
Audit trails must record all critical changes to data.

Section 5: System Validation
Computerized systems must be validated to demonstrate fitness for intended use.
