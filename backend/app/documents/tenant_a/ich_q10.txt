TITLE: ICH Q10 Pharmaceutical Quality System

Section 1: Introduction
The Pharmaceutical Quality System described in ICH Q10 provides a framework for a comprehensive system for pharmaceutical quality management.

Section 2: Management Responsibility
Senior management has the ultimate responsibility to ensure an effective pharmaceutical quality system is established and maintained.

Section 3: Continual Improvement
The system should facilitate continual improvement of product quality and process performance.

Section 4: Corrective and Preventive Actions (CAPA)
CAPA systems should investigate root causes of deviations and implement corrective actions.

Section 5: Change Management
Changes should be evaluated for potential impact on product quality and regulatory compliance.
