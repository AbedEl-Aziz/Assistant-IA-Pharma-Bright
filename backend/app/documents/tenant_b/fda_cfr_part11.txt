TITLE: FDA 21 CFR Part 11 – Electronic Records and Signatures

Section 1: Scope
This regulation applies to electronic records and electronic signatures.

Section 2: Electronic Records
Electronic records must be trustworthy, reliable, and equivalent to paper records.

Section 3: Electronic Signatures
Electronic signatures must be unique to one individual.

Section 4: Controls for Open Systems
Systems must ensure authenticity, integrity, and confidentiality of records.
